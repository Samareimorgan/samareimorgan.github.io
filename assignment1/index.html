About Me

Born in Englewood Colorado, Samantha has lived most of her life in the Denver Metro area. With an undergraduate degree from the University of Puget Sound in Tacoma, WA she began her career in Higher Education Administration at the Financial Aid Department at that University. Shortly after, she moved to Tacoma Community College, where she managed admission processes and created databases for data queries.

In 2004, Samantha moved back home to Colorado and spent 3 years as the Public Interest Practicum Coordinator at the University of Denver, Sturm College of Law. With 7 years coordinating and managing programs and data, Samantha was able to transfer those skills to office management in the Nonprofit sector.

Though her time in the nonprofit field was short, her desire to help people continues to shine through in her active involvement in the Rotary Club of Centennial, as current president-elect and volunteer for several committees. It is through the Rotary Club that she also discovered her passion of creating a diverse, welcoming and inclusive society. Her participation in the Rotary Action Group for Peace and her international ties allow her to live her passion.

Most recently, Samantha spent 7 years in the mortage industry with various roles from processor, Loan Officer Assistant, and Marketing Technologies Specialst. Her transition into a technologies position within the mortgage industry allowed her to discover a second passion: web development. With the completion of the University of Denver Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, Samantha hopes to be able to combine her two passions.

In her spare time, Samantha works as a freelance marketer and is building her passion project: The Code-4-Peace Initiative.
